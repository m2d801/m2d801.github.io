Toronto Maple Leafs Fast Facts

  1. The Toronto Maple Leafs team was formed in 1917. They were originally named the Toronto Blue Shirts, but changed their name to the Toronto Arenas. In 1919, they underwent their second name change to become the Toronto St. Patricks, and in 1926, they changed it a third time, creating the famous moniker that we all know and love today.
  2. The Toronto Maple Leafs was one of the six original hockey teams in the NHL.
  3. The Toronto Maple Leafs won their first Stanley Cup at the end of their first season, but this title is not counted due to the team having a different name back then.
